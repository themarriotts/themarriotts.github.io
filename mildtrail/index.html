RUGBY CAMRA MILD TRAIL 2001

For the past 3 years the good chaps at Rugby CAMRA

have organised a Mild Trail to promote this excellent beer.

The general idea is to visit each of the pubs on the list and

have a PINT of the Mild on offer, collect all 10 and you get a

'T' Shirt for the full SP take a look at Rugby CAMRA's web site.

This site will chart the progress of 3 Hillmorton beer drinkers on the trail
