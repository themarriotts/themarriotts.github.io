Our Local

The Stag & Pheasant

School Street Hillmorton

 This excellent old village local has been given a new lease of life under the caring management of Rachel and Mark Reynolds since their arrival earlier this summer. The traditional bar, featuring bare floorboards and high backed settles, is complemented by the cosy lounge.

As well as the regular staple beers of Ansells Mild, Ansells Bitter and Pedigree there has been an excellent variation of guest beers in recent months including: -

Lady Godiva

King Maker

Golden Bear

St Patrick

Triumph

Titanic
