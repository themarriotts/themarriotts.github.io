The Black Horse Walcote

Unofficial Homepage

I have created these pages in my little corner of cyber space show, that in these barren times of bland Nitro Keg beer and pre-packed, deep frozen , de-hydrated Pub Meals, there is are a refreshing oasis, where you can still get good beer and freshly prepared food.

I have been frequenting the Black Horse at Walcote for the last twenty years or more and other than that, I have no other connection with them. All the views comments and material found on this site is mine alone. I receive no financial reward for hosting this site.

If you are looking of the 'Real McCoy' when it comes to oriental food then look no further. The Landlady is Thai herself and cooks the food in an authentic and traditional style. Her major claim to fame is that she once cooked a meal for the Thai Royal Family. A good selection of Thai dishes can be found on the bar menu with there traditional Thai names.

 

The beer is very well kept and there is always a good choice of brews. The regulars include Hoskins & Oldfield 'HOB' as well as Hook Norton 'Old Hooky'. The guests are to numerous to mention here, however I will have a 'Guest Ale' page on the site in the near future. Apart form the beer there is also draft cider as well as a choice of bottled ciders. While I am on the contents of the bar it is worth mentioning the comprehensive selection of country fruit wines.
